About Evergreen Academy

Our Story & Values

Founded in 1998, Evergreen Academy began as a neighborhood initiative focused on holistic education. Over the years, we've grown into a respected K-12 institution, guided by a consistent set of principles.

Our Mission: To provide a balanced and challenging education that empowers students to become thoughtful, capable, and compassionate individuals.

Integrity

We encourage honesty and ethical decision-making in all aspects of school life.

Curiosity

We cultivate a love for learning that goes beyond textbooks and tests.

Community

We work in partnership with families and respect each child's unique journey.

Resilience

We provide the support needed to overcome challenges and grow from them.

Why Families Choose Evergreen

The Foundations of Trust

Our faculty are experienced, qualified, and deeply committed to student development. They are accessible to students and parents.
